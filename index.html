PROJECT IDEA

My project is to create an app that simulates the process of a surgery and allows user to perform a virtual surgery on it. The app could be run on a VR environment or just simply on a computer or a smart device. If in Virtual Reality, users will have to be equipped with VR headsets and our VR surgery kit. The application would be able to simulate real human bodies with different illnesses or damaged areas that needs surgery.

Although this idea is not original since there are many companies that already applied Virtual Reality to train their surgeons, it is not popular in Vietnam to use this technology in the medical industry. Therefore, I would like to develop a pure Vietnamese app for Vietnamese people, medical students, and doctors to learn and practice. My goal is to provide a convenient and efficient method as an alternative way to learn about surgery that does not requires a real human body.

As mentioned before, the application would be available on both PC and mobile platforms and provides a VR option. A VR surgery kit would also be developed to support the virtual experiences of users. The kit would contain interactive replicas of surgery knives, scissors, etc. which are fundamental tools when performing an operation. The first key feature of the app would be the ability to simulate human bodies of different age, gender, height and weight as your patient. Of course, the generated body would not be precise since different people has different body proportions, we will do research and get the average proportions of each group of age and gender. Therefore, creating bodies that are closest to the real ones. The second key feature is the ability to recreate blood, flesh and organs of the patient in the most realistic way using 3D modeling. This would require deep research of how the human body works and investments on the 3D developing field. The third key feature is the ability to mimic real illnesses and simulate damaged parts/organs of the patient. This feature is the core feature since user will mainly be interacting and operating surgeries on the damaged parts of the body. When it comes to using the application, the user will be asked for inputting parameters of the patient (gender, age, weight, height) and select the kind of sickness that they are dealing with (mainly the ones that requires a surgery). After that, the system will generate a virtual surgery room with the patient and let user to start the operation. Time limitation will be provided base on each kind of illness as well as heart rate/breath rate machine to monitor the current state of the patient. The operation will success if the user does the right procedures in the right amount of time and fails if they exceed the time limit or make critical mistakes. There will be two modes of the app, one is learning mode which gives user instructions on how to perform the surgery. The other mode would be practice mode that provides no instructions but a grading system on how well the operation has been done. Levels from normal (easy) to extremely serious (hard) cases would be provided for user to select base on their medical experience. We will also be supporting users with videos and documentations about many kinds of illness for a better learning experience. Since there are operations that needs two or more surgeons, our app will provide a collab session for user to connect and work together in one surgery via Wi-Fi or an online server. For the purpose of education, after a user accomplishes a certain milestone (a combination of many surgeries), they will receive our certificate that could be worthy in the future.

For the developing process, I think Unity would be the best option to build the application since it is one of the most reliable game/simulation developing software that supports both PC and mobile platforms. VR is also supported on Unity which makes the most important part of the application. VR consoles in form of surgery kits such as knifes, scissors, pliers, etc. should also be researched and produced to assist the user and make it become more realistic. Furthermore, deep research about human organs, illnesses and how to perform surgeries on different kind of problems that patients have would be the core feature that we focus on.

The skills required for this project would be programming, teamworking and researching. Because Unity is used to develop the app, we would practice the programming language C# since it is the main language that is used in the software. For this is an extremely challenging project that requires a lot of effort and research, we will need to work as a team of many members, each person will have different tasks. Therefore, teamworking skills are crucial for the group to work smoothly.

If this project become successful, it will create opportunities for medical students to practice surgery on unreal bodies, which is a lot more convenient and cost less than performing on an actual corpse. This way will help students to learn faster since they can do multiple surgeries in one day. Surgeons could also use our app to practice before a real operation so that it could be done precisely and increase the successful rate. Finally, our goal is to improve the medical industry of Vietnam, doctors will be more skillful so that more lives would be saved.

IT TECHNOLOGIES

Virtual/Augmented Reality

These are by far the two most advanced technologies that human can use to interact with computers. With the VR technology, it allows us to enter a whole different world that is created inside the of computer (with the support with VR gears such as VR glasses, headsets, etc.). User will enter a virtual environment that is made to look as vivid and lively as the real world. This virtual environment is generated by the computer itself using 3D rendering. In most cases, the user will need to have a VR headset connected to a computer or a smart device to use this technology. Nowadays, this tech is most commonly used to develop games and simulations. To be inside of a game and play it just like the real word is every gamers’ dream. It brings a lot of excitement and whole new experiences to gamers while traveling inside the gaming world. Some of the most popular VR games are Beat Saber, Half-Life: Alyx, SUPERHOT. I have tried playing VR Beat Saber before and can tell that it is a very well-developed game and very fun to play. Furthermore, this technology can involve in a lot of other industries such as aviation where they use VR flight simulators to train pilots, or even in the education industry where students can explore new knowledges and see the outside world within their classroom.

The AR technology on the other hand is slightly different from VR since it does not provide an entirely virtual experience but rather generates what is called a mixed reality. This kind of reality is created by using a computer to view the real world. Applying cognitive science and 3D rendering, the computer will be able to scan the outside world using a camera and add more details to it base on the purpose of the application. For instance, Pokemon Go is once a very popular game that uses AR in its operating system. User can view their current position on its map and see nearby pokemons. When comes to capturing a pokemon, user will have to open their camera and that pokemon will appear inside the camera just like how it is in the real world. This kind of tech could be effectively used in a lot of different industries to display more information. For example, lots of fashion brands have offered AR for user to try-on their products with the precision of 70-80% like they are trying clothes at the real store. Maybe in the future, human will be able to create a whole different reality that looks and feels just like real life.

Although it is still not very common in Vietnam to see these two technologies applying in real life circumstances other than gaming due to its very high price and can cause inconvenience to user (big headsets, dizziness after using for a long period of time). I believe that AR/VR will continuously be developed for better user experience and will become an extremely dominant technology in the future. It would bring a huge impact to the entertainment industry when customers do not have to actually go to a certain place such as cinemas, museums to watch movies or see art, but enjoying all those services remotely at home. In a near future, labor in all industry would be equipped with a pair of smart glasses that scans their surroundings and display work-related information.

Until now, these technologies have not brought any significant impact to my daily life, other than offering more gaming options.

Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

Artificial Intelligence is the term for the process of mimicking human behavior of computers. Ever since the creation of human, we have always been improving our labor tools from time to time, decades through decades. Until these days, technology has shifted the way we work, and scientists are searching for a way that machines could do all the work for humans, brings the definition of Artificial Intelligence. Computers can be taught to think like human by many methods, one of them is Machine Learning. Loads of data will be provided to computers to generate patterns or useful information using algorithms developed by humans. For instance, when using the world’s largest music streaming platform – Spotify, the application will monitor user’s behavior like what music genre they listen to the most, and then generates songs recommendations that fits the user’s interest. The process requires an amount of input information and through an algorithm, beneficial information will be extracted. The more powerful and well-developed the algorithm is, the more reliable and valuable the output statistics would be. Applying AI to robots, we can create machines that have “brains” and can interact with human. Sophia, a robot developed by Hanson Robotics is considered the smartest robot in the world since she can communicate and display facial expressions just like a normal person. Furthermore, automation in many aspects of our daily life is being developed using AI. Tesla have created the auto-pilot mode for their electronic cars that is said to assist drivers when driving. The car will be able to scan its surroundings for vehicles, road signs and other obstacles, and automatically. The car also can detect for danger, if seeing potentials of an accident it would emergency brake or drift to another direction. Although the driver must put their hands on the wheels throughout the drive since we cannot rely entirely on machines, it still brings lots of convenience to the driver and enhances road safety. Creation of virtual assistants such as Alexa or Siri is also the application of AI. These non-human assistants can communicate well with us and can perform simple tasks like scheduling or search something on your phones. In the future, robots would inherit human’s intelligence and grow even smarter than our kind. Machines then would replace human to work since they cannot feel tired and leads to the burst of work efficiency in every industry. Of course, to get machine to think just like us, they will need a lot of technology enhancements to help deep learning, have cognitive behavior and the ability to process information continuously for better decision making.

The greatest impact of Artificial Intelligence is making human’s life easier. More and more of the stuff we use in our daily life are being automated such as vacuum robots, fridge, washing machines. Many industries have already used robots to do some simple to complex works that are assisted and monitored by human. By using technology, we do not have to put so much effort in working like how it is before. Automation helps us perform work faster and workers do not have to know how to make the product but rather learn how to use machines. Dangerous tasks could now be done by machines which keeps the workforce safe and less tired. This leads to the rise in productivity but could also cause some downsides. Since machines can do all the work, it would leave people unemployed, poverty rate would then rise. Studies have shown that more and more job are being replaced by automation. For example, front desk works such as customer consulting or support could be easily done by a well-developed virtual assistant or chatbot. Human could also be relying so much on machines that they may lose many crucial life skills. Some health problems such as obesity would appear if people do not physically work or exercise enough.

In my daily life, AI helps me do house chores such as cleaning house using vacuum robot, washing clothes using an automated machine that requires only putting clothes in and push the start button. It indeed has helped me saved a lot of time and effort and I could use my time to do other stuff such as studying or entertainment. With entertaining purposes, algorithms in platforms like YouTube, TikTok or Spotify have always giving out relevant video and music recommendations so that I do not have to find myself crawling through the website/application to look for the content that fits my interests.
